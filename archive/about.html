About Us

Anjali Mehta, RCIC

Anjali Mehta is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) and a Postgraduate in Human Resources with extensive professional experience across human resources management, public relations, and immigration consultation. With a strong foundation in people-oriented roles and regulatory processes, she has developed a client-centered approach that emphasizes clarity, empathy, and integrity.

Before establishing CanDen Immigration Services, Anjali worked in various HR and PR capacities where she honed her skills in communication, case analysis, documentation, and client relations. These experiences have equipped her with the ability to navigate complex immigration pathways while ensuring a smooth and supportive experience for her clients.

As a licensed RCIC, Anjali provides honest assessments, strategic guidance, and thorough application support across permanent and temporary immigration programs. She is committed to upholding the highest standards of professional ethics while helping individuals, families, and businesses achieve their Canadian immigration goals.

Professional Background &
Expertise

Anjali Mehta brings a unique combination of human resources expertise and immigration consulting credentials to her practice. Her background in HR has provided her with deep insights into workforce dynamics, employment relationships, and the professional needs of individuals seeking to build careers in Canada.

Her experience in public relations has strengthened her ability to communicate complex immigration policies clearly and effectively, ensuring clients understand their options and the processes involved. This multidisciplinary approach allows her to provide comprehensive support that addresses both the technical requirements of immigration applications and the personal aspects of relocating to a new country.
